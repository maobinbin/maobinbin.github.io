```html
1.Start an enumerated list, by selecting Enumerate from the drop down menu.

2.Add an empty ERT-box at the start of the item, move the cursor outside it and hit Enter.

3.Press Tab. This starts a sublist, which is numbered with letters.

4.Pressing Enter starts a new item in the sublist, to make this a new item on the main list, hit Shift + Tab, or hit Enter a second time.
```
